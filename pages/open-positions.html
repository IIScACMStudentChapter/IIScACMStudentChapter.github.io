Tenure

Count

Activity Co-ordinator (AC)

Responsibilities
- Broadly responsible for managing the lifecycle of an assigned event. The co-ordinatior is expected to, using the appropriate chanels, publicize the event, manage logistics, gather and submit relevant statistics and finances. Being at the centre stage of organizing an event will equip candidates interested in playing a bigger role in the Chapter's operations with hands on experience.
Tenure
- Duration of the event
Count
- One AC per event
